Learn More Register Now

Why Brainhack?

Brainhack is a collaborative event that focuses on the open sharing of ideas, code, and data. During Brainhack Global, we will meet in Montreal to work on projects in coordination with other Brainhack sites around the world. Join us on Mattermost to connect with the community and start planning your project! Our channel name is #brainhack-global-mtl


Dates: December 4-6, 2019
Cost: C$30 for registration with onsite breakfast & lunch
Location: Concordia University Conference Centre, John Molson School of Business (9th floor), 1600 Boulevard de Maisonneuve Ouest, Montreal, QC, H3H 1J5
